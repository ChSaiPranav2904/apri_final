About Liver Health

The liver plays a critical role in metabolism, detoxification, and digestion. Monitoring liver function is vital for early detection of conditions such as fibrosis and cirrhosis.

What is Pathology?

Pathology is the study of disease. It involves examining tissues, cells, and organs to understand the nature and cause of diseases, including liver damage.

About This Project

This APRI Calculator was developed by students of BITS Pilani, Hyderabad Campus, under the guidance of doctors from Yashoda Hospitals. It helps estimate liver fibrosis levels using accessible blood test parameters.
